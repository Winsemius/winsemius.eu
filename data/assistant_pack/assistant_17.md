## InvestEU Defence Equity Facility

- Issuer: European Investment Fund
- Country: EU
- Language: en
- Tags: eu;public-finance;defence;equity;investeu
- Source: https://www.eif.org/flagship-initiatives/investeu/defence-equity-facility

### Kernpunten
- [sectie: investing in companies developing innovative defence technologies.] Launched in January 2024, the new €175 million Defence Equity Facility (DEF) under InvestEU is managed by the EIF on behalf of the European Commission (Directorate General for Defence Industry and Space – DEFIS).
- [sectie: investing in companies developing innovative defence technologies.] DEF’s resources have been provided as follows: a €100 million commitment from the European Defence Fund and an additional €75 million from the EIF . The funding period has been set for the next four years - currently until 2027, expecting to mobilise up to €500 million in investments at the level of beneficiary companies, including private sector participation.
- [sectie: investing in companies developing innovative defence technologies.] The facility forms part of the EIB Group’s strategic priority to support the EU’s security and defence objectives, including other EIB initiatives such as the Strategic European Security Initiative (SESI) .
- [sectie: referenced links] - https://eudis.europa.eu/defence-equity-facility_en
- [sectie: investing in companies developing innovative defence technologies.] The Defence Equity Facility aims to support venture capital and private equity funds that are investing in European companies developing innovative defence technologies with a ‘dual-use’ potential. Investments under DEF, as is the case for all EIF operations, are subject to compliance with the EIF’s Policy on exclusion and restrictions .
- [sectie: how to apply?] Further information, including indicative term sheets and product descriptions are available under the equity products page. Fund managers seeking support from the Facility should apply through this website.
