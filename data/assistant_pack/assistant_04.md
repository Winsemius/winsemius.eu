# Assistant Pack 04 - Submission Checklist

## Standard checklist (cross-program)
- Verify applicant/legal entity eligibility and country rules.
- Confirm TRL start/end and technical workplan fit.
- Confirm funding rate, co-financing and eligible cost categories.
- Identify consortium requirements (mandatory partners, countries).
- Collect mandatory annexes/templates before drafting.
- Validate call deadlines and submission portal constraints.
- Prepare evaluation evidence: impact, implementation, risk, exploitation.
- Cross-check proposal against official call topic wording.
